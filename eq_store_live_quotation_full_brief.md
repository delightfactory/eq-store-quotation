# القسم العاشر — مسودة ملف Skill المقترح مبدئيًا

> هذه مسودة يمكن تعديلها بعد مراجعة Claude.

```md
# INTERACTIVE_QUOTATION_SKILL.md

## Mission

Build a premium RTL Arabic interactive quotation experience for EQ Store, issued by Delight Factory for Car Care Products, with digital experience powered by Tech Edge.

The page must present a formal bulk manufacturing quotation while also demonstrating a modern live budget monitoring concept. The experience should feel like a luxury B2B manufacturing dashboard, not a shop, catalog, or ordinary quotation table.

## Project Identity

- Quotation issuer: Delight Factory for Car Care Products.
- Client: EQ Store.
- Digital experience: Powered by Tech Edge.
- Product type: Bulk liquid car care products.
- Delivery scope: Ex-factory / delivered at Delight Factory.
- Quotation total: 503,125 EGP.
- Validity: 3 business days from issue date.
- Date: 29 April 2026.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

1. Do not make the page look like an e-commerce store.
2. Do not add cart, checkout, buy buttons, product ratings, or consumer shopping patterns.
3. The quotation is issued by Delight Factory, not Tech Edge.
4. Tech Edge may appear only as the digital experience provider.
5. Prices are bulk manufacturing prices, not retail prices.
6. Prices exclude VAT.
7. Prices exclude packaging, filling, labeling, printing, cartons, transportation, and external supplies unless explicitly stated.
8. Maintain a clear distinction between official quoted prices and future reviewed/current prices.
9. Do not silently alter the official quotation total.
10. Any future price update must be shown as Current Reviewed Price or Budget Impact, not as a change to the issued quotation.
11. Use Arabic RTL as the primary layout direction.
12. Ensure all Arabic copy is clean, formal, and commercially precise.

## UX Principles

- The first 10 seconds must answer: who issued the quotation, who receives it, what is the total, what is being supplied, and why the allocation is strategic.
- Use a dashboard-style executive summary before detailed product tables.
- Prioritize clarity over decoration.
- Use progressive disclosure for long product descriptions.
- Keep financial figures readable and highly visible.
- Show the quotation as a formal commercial document, but present it through a modern interactive experience.
- Allow the user to understand the allocation strategy before reading every product detail.

## Required Page Sections

1. Quotation Hero
2. Executive Metrics Dashboard
3. Strategic Budget Allocation
4. Official Quotation Table
5. Product Detail Cards
6. Raw Material Cost Review Timeline
7. Live Budget Impact Monitor
8. Production Progress Tracker Placeholder
9. Commercial Terms & Notes
10. Final Summary / Next Steps
11. Footer Branding

## Visual Direction

Use a Luxury Automotive B2B Dashboard style:

- Dark graphite backgrounds.
- Metallic gold accents for financial and premium highlights.
- Subtle glassmorphism cards.
- Clean data tables.
- Premium progress indicators.
- Elegant charts for category allocation.
- Motion should be subtle, professional, and not distracting.
- Avoid childish colors, generic SaaS templates, and cluttered corporate layouts.

## Color Suggestions

- Background: graphite, deep navy, near-black.
- Primary accents: gold, champagne, bronze.
- Secondary accents: silver, cyan, muted green, dark red for categories.
- Premium Graphite section: black, carbon, sharp highlights.

## Data Rules

All quotation data should be stored in one central static data file, preferably:

`src/data/quotationData.ts`

The data file should contain:

- quotation metadata
- client details
- products
- quantities
- quoted prices
- calculated totals
- category grouping
- raw material review dates
- current reviewed prices placeholder
- production status placeholder

All totals should be calculated from the data where practical, not hard-coded across many components.

## Pricing and Versioning Rules

- Official quotation version starts at Version 1.0.
- Official quotation total: 503,125 EGP.
- Show current reviewed prices only as a separate monitoring layer.
- Budget impact = current estimated total - official quotation total.
- If no current reviewed price exists, current price equals quoted price.
- Any future change should show direction: increase, decrease, unchanged.
- Do not overwrite historical quoted price.

## Component Requirements

Recommended components:

- `QuotationHero`
- `ExecutiveMetrics`
- `BudgetAllocation`
- `OfficialQuotationTable`
- `ProductCards`
- `ProductCard`
- `RawMaterialTimeline`
- `LiveBudgetMonitor`
- `ProductionTracker`
- `CommercialTerms`
- `FooterBranding`

Each component should be readable, reusable, and data-driven.

## Product Card Requirements

Each product card must show:

- product name
- category
- role
- quantity
- price per kg
- total value
- strategic role
- key strengths
- reason for inclusion

Long descriptions should be expandable or visually separated to avoid clutter.

## RTL and Arabic Requirements

- Set `dir="rtl"` at root level.
- Use Arabic-friendly font such as IBM Plex Sans Arabic, Cairo, or Tajawal.
- Keep numbers readable, preferably using Latin digits for financial clarity.
- Align tables and labels correctly in RTL.
- Avoid broken punctuation between Arabic and English.

## Responsive Requirements

- Mobile-first responsive behavior.
- Tables should become cards or horizontally scrollable on small screens.
- KPI cards should stack cleanly on mobile.
- Product cards should remain readable without overwhelming the user.
- Charts and progress bars must not break on mobile.

## What Not To Do

- Do not create a shop layout.
- Do not overuse animations.
- Do not hide key financial totals deep inside the page.
- Do not make Tech Edge look like the quotation issuer.
- Do not remove the commercial notes.
- Do not merge quoted price and current reviewed price.
- Do not use playful visuals or consumer discount language.
- Do not add VAT or packaging costs unless explicitly provided.

## QA Checklist

Before delivery, verify:

- [ ] The page clearly says the quotation is issued by Delight Factory.
- [ ] The client name EQ Store is visible.
- [ ] The total 503,125 EGP is accurate.
- [ ] Product totals match quantity × price.
- [ ] Category allocation percentages are accurate.
- [ ] VAT exclusion is clearly shown.
- [ ] Packaging/filling/labeling exclusion is clearly shown.
- [ ] Quotation validity is 3 business days.
- [ ] Raw material review dates are included.
- [ ] Live Budget Monitor separates official and current prices.
- [ ] Production Tracker is marked as a post-approval tracking area.
- [ ] RTL works on desktop and mobile.
- [ ] No e-commerce elements exist.
- [ ] Footer correctly states: Issued by Delight Factory / Digital Experience by Tech Edge.
```
